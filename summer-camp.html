The Program

What You'll Experience

🏗️

Business Incubator Lab

Develop your own business idea — individually or in teams — inside an incubator-styled environment designed to mirror how real startups are built.

🚀

Entrepreneurship Workshops

Learn how real businesses are built: branding, pricing, customer discovery, pitching, and product development — taught by people who've done it.

💰

Finance Workshops

Understand money beyond the basics — budgeting, investing, wealth-building principles, and financial decision-making that actually sticks.

🎨

Creative Expression Block

Explore product design, visual storytelling, or arts-based entrepreneurship pathways. Creativity is a business skill here.

⛺

Outdoor Recreation

Led by university staff to let out some steam, reset your mind, and build team energy. Because the best ideas come when you're not staring at a screen.

Your Takeaways

By the End of Camp, You'll:

✓

Pitch a business idea you developed from concept to presentation

✓

Understand core financial principles that most adults wish they'd learned earlier

✓

Strengthen teamwork and leadership skills in real-world collaborative settings

✓

Build confidence in your decision-making — in business and in life

✓

Leave with a clearer vision for your future and the tools to pursue it
